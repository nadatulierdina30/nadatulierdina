My Playlist

MESSAGE IN A BOTTLE

"Message in a Bottle” is a song by Taylor Swift, an American singer-songwriter. It is the twenty-fifth track on Swift's second re-recorded album, Red (Taylor's Version), and was released by Republic Records (2021). Swift, Max Martin, and Shellback wrote the song, with the latter also producing it alongside Elvira Anderfjärd. In November 2021, it was released as a single on US pop and adult pop radio stations.


PERMISSION TO DANCE

Ed Sheeran, Johnny McDaid, Steve Mac, and Jenna Andrews wrote "Permission to Dance," which was produced by Mac and Andrews alongside Stephen Kirk. In the United States, "Permission to Dance" debuted at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 on July 24, 2021, with 15.9 million streams, 1.1 million radio airplay impressions, and 140,000 copies sold in the first week.


MEMORIES

"Memories" is the lead single from Maroon 5's seventh studio album Jordi, which was released on September 20, 2019, through 222 and Interscope Records. The song's lyrics pay tribute to the memories of a loved one who has since passed away.


HAPPIER

"Happier" (stylized in all lowercase) is a song by Olivia Rodrigo, an American singer-songwriter. It was included as the eighth track on Rodrigo's debut studio album Sour, which was released by Geffen Records on May 21, 2021. Rodrigo wrote the song entirely on his own, with Dan Nigro handling the production.

Over a deep base production, "Happier" is a folk-pop piano ballad. The song's lyrics describe the difficulties of moving on after a breakup while selfishly wishing their ex-partner was unhappy in their new relationship. When Sour was released, music critics praised the song, with many describing it as one of the album's best songs and highlights.
